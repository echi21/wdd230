News Release

An analysis by the Argentine Chamber of Commerce and Services (CAC) shows that, in 2021, the commercial exchange between Argentina and Ecuador was 872 million dollars, 61.2% higher than in 2020. In this way, bilateral trade returned to growing after two consecutive years of decline, reaching its highest historical level by exceeding the previous record of 2018 of 725 million dollars by 12.7%.
